Our Facilities

The student-faculty ratio at Stony Brook University--SUNY is 18:1, and the school has 43.2 percent of its classes with fewer than 20 students. The most popular majors at Stony Brook University--SUNY include: Health Professions and Related Programs; Biological and Biomedical Sciences; Engineering; Psychology; and Business, Management, Marketing, and Related Support Services. The average freshman retention rate, an indicator of student satisfaction, is 90 percent.

At Uname, students have the opportunity to integrate theory and practice in the classroom and state-of-the art facilities. This synergy between fundamental and applied research is demonstrated by the ongoing work at more than 100 research centers and institutes, with investigations into issues ranging from the decentralization of politics to the inner workings of financial markets, to the psychology of computer games and the study of how tumors invade the body’s immune system.

The Uname is one of the best in the nation at creating startup companies based on student and faculty research. Innovation Node focused on teaching entrepreneurial scientists, from all fields of Life Sciences, Engineering, and math sciences the skills needed to bring proprietary technology to market and improving the odds of startup success.They are widely considered to be a proven tool to encourage the formation of innovative high technology companies.
